Welcome to CodeBuddies!

What is CodeBuddies?

CodeBuddies was founded two years ago by a group of passionate programming beginners who set out to learn together and support each other. The idea arose from the necessity of having a place where beginners in programming could ask their questions, exchange ideas, and grow together.

How you will learn!

Weekly Study Groups:

Various groups, based on programming languages or technologies, meet regularly to go through tutorials together and engage in practical exercises.

Coding Challenges:

Monthly coding challenges are organized to challenge members and enhance their skills. There are prizes and recognition for those who present outstanding solutions.

Project Collaborations:

Members have the opportunity to sign up for group projects to work on real applications. This not only promotes practical learning but also encourages teamwork.

Guest Lectures:

Experienced developers and programmers are invited to deliver inspiring lectures and share their experiences in the field of software development.
